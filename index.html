Why Lumbar Pain is a Growing Problem

In today’s digital world, many people spend hours sitting at desks, driving, or looking at screens. This sedentary lifestyle leads to poor posture and back issues, especially in the lumbar region (lower back).

Common Causes of Lumbar Pain:


  • Poor posture

  • Sedentary lifestyle

  • Injuries or accidents

  • Muscle strain

  • Herniated discs

  • Sciatica
